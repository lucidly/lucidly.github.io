About

Hi! My name is Lily, but I'm most well known as Lucid or Lucidly on the internet. It's a lot easier to find me this way.

I have a Bachelors of Science in Interactive Arts + Technology, with concentrations in Design and Interactive Systems. It means I've studied a lot of different design skills with some programming.

I specialize in creating design work that often has a large illustration component to it. I am mostly interested in making games, designing for the web, and of course, creating illustrations.

My work is inspired by Japanese cute culture and modern design trends. It is often simple with a subtle cute feeling.

Other than that, I like to play games! I am an achiever and explorer who spends time acquiring massive amounts of wealth by playing with virtual economies and exploring the limits of every map. I enjoy MMOs and RPGs the most.

If you want to get to know me better, I'd love to become friends! As Hello Kitty says, you can never have too many friends.
