About

Hi! My name is Lily, but I'm known as Lucid or Lucidly on the internet. It's a lot easier to find me this way.

My work is inspired by Japanese cute culture and modern design trends. I specialize in creating design work that often has a large illustration component to it.

I have a Bachelors of Science in Interactive Arts + Technology, with concentrations in Design and Interactive Systems.

Other than my work, I also like to play games, mainly MMOs. I am an achiever and explorer who spends time acquiring titles, playing with virtual economies, and finding hard to reach places.
