About


Hi! My name is Lily, but I go by Lucid or Lucidly on the internet. It's a lot easier to find me this way since you can use @lucidly on most websites to reach me.

I specialize in creating design work that often has a large illustration component to it. My work is inspired by Japanese cute culture and modern design trends. It is often simple with a delicate and cute feeling.

As for other interests, I also like to play games! I am an achiever and explorer who plays MMOs and RPGs. You can find me in games acquiring massive amounts of wealth, collecting costumes, and trying to get past boundary boxes to explore hidden areas.

If you want to get to know me better, I'd love to become friends!
