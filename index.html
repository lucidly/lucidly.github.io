About



Hi! My name is Lily, although I prefer going by Lucidly (often shortened to Lucid) on the internet. It's a lot easier to find me this way, trust me!

I'm an illustrator and designer that can create cute illustrations, write code, design websites, and develop games.

I have a Bachelor of Science in Interactive Arts + Technology, with concentrations in Design and Interactive Systems.

My dream is to make the world a better place with the power of cuteness! I believe cuteness can bring joy even in the unlikeliest of places.

Aside from my work, I like playing games. Especially MMOs and RPGs! You can find me in games acquiring massive amounts of wealth, collecting costumes, and exploring hidden areas.

If you want to get to know me better, I'd love to become friends!
